Our Story

Our Story

Xocora is dedicated to creating and providing the ultimate in luxury gifts for all age groups. We use only fairly traded, organically grown, premium quality products sourced from small-scale farmers, supporting farming communities through trade rather than aid.

Quality doesn’t come from taste alone. At Xocora we pride ourselves on knowing that our cocoa beans are sustainably and ethically sourced. That’s why we implemented our own sustainable sourcing model: The Xocora Farming Program.
